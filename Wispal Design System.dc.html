Brand · 10 — Voice & tone
A quiet friend, never a coach
Every line is templated from content packs (no LLM). The voice is warm, plain-spoken, and structurally incapable of shame — because shame is what the category leader sells.
PRINCIPLES
Present, not pushy
It sits beside you. It suggests; it never demands or counts.
No shame, ever
A missed day is met with relief, not guilt. Bond never drops.
Moments, not minutes
Celebrate the finished thing — never the hours logged.
Rest is a win
When you've done enough, it says so — and means it.
SAY THIS, NOT THAT
WELCOME BACK ✓
"Hey, good to see you. Want to do a little?"
NOT
"You broke your 6-day streak!"
FATIGUE ✓
"You've done plenty today. Resting is the win now."
NOT
"Just one more hour to beat your record!"
COMPLETE ✓
"That's the chapter done. Nice one."
NOT
"+25 min! You're #14 on the leaderboard."
Lowercase, contractions, short. Vietnamese-English code-switching is welcome in VN voice packs.
